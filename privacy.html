Privacy Policy for Trex Bot

In Trex Bot, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by TrexBot and how we use it.

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.

Consent

By using our bot, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.

Information we collect

In Discord we access your member-id, username,guild-id and channel-id in your server. This is requiered for functioning of certain commands, and you may opt out from storage of this data and request deletion.

How we use your information

We use the information we collect in various ways, including:

  • To maintain leaderboards, economy accounts, levels accounts and for keeping track of your AntiGhost, AntiSwear and AutoRole systems.
  • If AntiGhost is setup, TrexBot tracks whether messages are edited/deleted to prevent ghost pinging. You may opt out from this tracking by deleting the setup for your server
  • If Bot-conversation is setup, TrexBot sends your chat content, of the specific channel to ChatGPT API. You may opt out from this tracking by deleting the setup for your server
  • If AntiSwear is setup, TrexBot checks your chat content and verifies that no swear words are used. You may opt out from this tracking by deleting the setup for your server. We do not review any of the chat content, it is software that does it. We also do not store or share this content to any other platforms.
  • If polls are used, TrexBot counts the number of reactions to count votes. We do not store this anywhere. You may opt out by not reacting to polls.
  • We require your prescence data to show the count of online members when /server is used. We only check for dnd,invisible and online status. This information is not stored anywhere or tracked. It is just displayed at an instant when the command is used.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us by joining the Support Server.
